Making bakery-worthy chocolate chip cookies is much easier than it seems. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons hot water

Direction

  1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
